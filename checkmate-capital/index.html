Checkmate Capital is an investment group managing family office assests and Asian-Pacific institutional fund. Checkmate's primary areas of investment are energy and energy technology, biomedical and biotechnology, and diversified special situations opportunities.


With offices in Los Angeles, Beijing and an affiliate office in Singapore, Checkmate's partners provides seasoned and diverse expertise in their relevant industries as well as leverage a powerfully efficient international ecosystem of strategic partners, business talent and resources.


Typically long-term focused, Checkmate's investment approach embodies strategic oversight and an ownership paradigm. Where most investment funds end is where are applied expertise in management and strategic acceleration begins.

our approach

Checkmate's Capital combines strong portfolio infrastructure with internal with internal industry leadership to create or advance special situation investment opportunities and alliances that can be advanced using strategic aspects of the Checkmate's platform.


With longer-term focus than typical investment groups, our active-partner approach generates attractive returns over an extented time horizon.

our expertise

Utilizing the Checkmate Approach will enable our investors (and the companies in which we invest) to leverage multiple synergies and complementary relationships across Checkmate's technology spectrum.

Checkmate's equity acquisition strategy provides for downside protection risk mitigation and upside potential.
